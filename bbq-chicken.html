Dr.Pepper's BBQ Chicken

Description

Perfect for serving over a nice baked potato or as part of a sandwich or just as an entree for the right sides.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz. Dr. Pepper
  • 1 tsp. Onion Powder
  • 1/4 tsp. Garlic Powder
  • 2 cups BBQ Sauce
  • 2+ lbs. Chicken Breasts

Cooking Steps

  1. Mix soda, BBQ sauce, and onion and garlic powders in a crock-pot
  2. Add chicken breasts to crock-pot
  3. Cover chicken with mixture
  4. Cook on low for 6 hrs
  5. Shred chicken and enjoy however you like
