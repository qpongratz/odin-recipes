Sweet and Sour Meatballs

Description

Perfect over a nice bed of hot rice

Ingredients

  • 28 oz. Meatballs
  • 1 cup Brown Sugar
  • 2 tbsp. Soy Sauce
  • 3 tbsp. Cornstarch
  • 2/3 cups Vinegar
  • 20 oz. Can Pineapple Chunks
  • Bag of frozen carrots (optional)

Cooking Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix
    • Brown Sugar
    • Vinegar
    • Soy Sauce
    • Cornstarch
    • Juice from Pineapple can
  2. Add meatballs to crock-pot
  3. Cover with liquid mixture
  4. Cover with pineapple chunks
  5. Cover with frozen carrots
  6. Cook on low for 2 hours
  7. Switch to high for 1 hour
  8. Enjoy
