Lasagna

Description

The perfect lasagna recipe for at home or on the go!

Ingredients

  • 12 Lasagna Noodles
  • 2 Cups Ricotta Cheese
  • 1/4 Cups Parsley
  • 1 Egg
  • 4 Cups Mozzarella
  • 1/2 Cups Parmesan
  • 36 oz. Pasta Sauce
  • 1 lb. Ground Meat
  • 1 tsp. Italian Seasoning
  • 1 tsp. Onion Powder
  • 1 tsp. Garlic Powder

Cooking Steps

  1. Cook your pasta noodles if they need cooking
  2. To prepare cheese mixture mix these Ingredients in a bowl
    • 2 Cups Ricotta Cheese
    • 1/4 Cups Parsley
    • 1 Egg
    • 1 and 1/2 Cups Mozzarella
    • 1/4 Cups Parmesan
  3. Brown your ground meat with the italian seasoning and onion and garlic powders
  4. Drain fat from meat an add sauce
  5. Stir and let simmer
  6. In baking dish, layer your lasagna as follows
    • 1 cup meat sauce
    • 3 noodles
    • 1/3 of the cheese mixture
    • Repeat until out of cheese mixture and finish off with last of noodles topped with last of meat sauce
  7. Cover with foil
  8. Bake at 350 in oven for 45 mins
  9. Remove and cover with remaining cheese (1/4 cup parmesan and 2 and 1/2 cups mozzarella)
  10. Bake another 10-15 mins
  11. Let cool for a few mins and then enjoy!
